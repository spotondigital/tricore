About Us

Engineering Smarter Engineering Solutions!

TriCore Power Systems (Pty) Ltd is a leading South African engineering and technology company, founded in 2025 by Menzi Njabulo Sibiya and Nompilo Lusanda Mabele. We specialize in delivering intelligent, sustainable power solutions tailored for industrial, mining, and commercial sectors. Our expertise ensures energy efficiency, operational reliability, and seamless automation for your business.

With a strong commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, TriCore Power Systems provides advanced electrical infrastructure, backup power systems, variable speed drives, and custom energy solutions designed to meet the evolving demands of modern industries. We partner with trusted suppliers and industry-leading technologies to deliver dependable results that drive productivity and long-term growth.

Our Vision
To become South Africa’s leading provider of intelligent and sustainable power control solutions.
Our Mission
To provide high-quality, energy-efficient electrical systems and services that meet the evolving needs of our clients. We are committed to innovation, technical excellence, and exceptional customer service.
